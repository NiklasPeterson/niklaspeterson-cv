### Overview

I had the opportunity to design the **Lummi Figma Plugin**, a tool that integrates seamlessly with Figma, allowing users to easily insert high-quality images from the Lummi platform directly into their design projects. My primary goal was to focus on **usability**, **simplicity**, and **consistency** with Lummi’s existing web experience while offering essential features like **quick image insertion**, **randomized image selection**, and the ability to explore **curated collections and creator profiles**.

This project aimed to streamline the process of finding and incorporating images into Figma, making it both intuitive for new users and powerful for experienced designers.

### Problem Statement

Lummi wanted to extend its web experience to a Figma plugin that allowed users to:

1. **Quickly search and insert images** without leaving their Figma workspace.

2. Maintain the **visual and interactive consistency** with the Lummi web platform.

3. Ensure **ease of use**, making it simple to navigate categories, creators, and collections.

4. Offer creators a way to **engage with their profiles** and **track statistics** like views and downloads within Figma.

My challenge was to bring these features into a minimalistic yet functional design while keeping the user flow as frictionless as possible.

### Design Process

#### 1. Research and User Personas

Understanding the needs of both **Figma users** and **Lummi creators** was the first step. The target users were primarily:

* **Designers** working within Figma who often need a library of images or assets.

* **Content creators** on Lummi who want visibility and engagement with their portfolio.

Through research, I identified key pain points:

* Designers needed an efficient way to **search and insert images**.

* The interface had to be **lightweight and fast** to prevent disrupting their workflow.

* Creators wanted more visibility and interaction with their **portfolios and stats**.

#### 2. Defining Core Features

The main features defined early on included:

* **Quick image insertion**: Users can search for specific images or randomly insert multiple images in one click.

* **Home Feed, Categories & Filters**: Users could easily navigate through different categories or use filters to refine searches.

* **Creator Profiles**: Creators can view profile stats such as downloads, views, and collection performance, ensuring transparency and engagement.

#### 3. Low-Fi Wireframes

I started with wireframes that focused on structuring the content in a familiar layout, consistent with Figma’s plugin architecture. Each section, from the search bar to the creator profiles, was designed to minimize cognitive load.

#### 4. High-Fidelity Design

After the wireframe stage, I transitioned to high-fidelity designs that matched **Lummi's visual identity**. Key design choices included:

* A **dark theme** to align with the aesthetic preferences of many designers using Figma.

* Clean, **minimalistic layouts** that ensured ease of navigation.

* Consistent **iconography and typography** from the Lummi brand to maintain familiarity.

#### 5. Prototype and Testing

I created an interactive prototype using Figma’s native prototyping tools. The prototype was tested with a small group of users, ranging from Figma beginners to seasoned designers, to gather feedback on ease of use and functionality.

### Key Features

1. **Home Feed & Categories**:

   * The home feed was designed to mimic Lummi’s web experience, offering users a variety of image suggestions upon opening the plugin.

   * Category navigation allows for easy discovery of images across different themes and genres.

2. **Quick Image Insertion**:

   * A simple one-click feature that inserts images directly into the Figma canvas.

   * Users can also opt to insert multiple random images, which can speed up the creative process.

3. **Filters & Search Functionality**:

   * Users can apply filters based on categories or search for images with keywords, making the experience streamlined and intuitive.

4. **Creator Profiles & Stats**:

   * Creators have access to a profile page that provides key metrics like **downloads**, **views**, and **image performance**.

   * This section was designed to encourage creators to engage more with the platform and understand the performance of their content.

### Final Design

The final design maintained a clean, dark interface, with an emphasis on ease of use and quick navigation. The result is a plugin that integrates seamlessly with Figma while providing all the functionality of the Lummi web experience, directly in the user’s design environment.

#### Visuals:

* The **home feed** provides users with a mix of suggestions, top downloads, and new arrivals.

* **Creator profiles** include key information and a way to connect via Instagram.

* **Quick access** to various categories and filters ensures that the user journey from search to insertion is effortless.

### Outcome

The Lummi Figma plugin enhances the creative workflow for designers by allowing quick, easy access to thousands of curated images. It has received positive feedback from both designers and content creators, and adoption rates have been strong, demonstrating the value of integrating Lummi's rich content library into a powerful design tool like Figma.
