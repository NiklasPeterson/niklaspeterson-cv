As the lead designer on Bueno Drops, I played a critical role in shaping the user experience and interface design for the plugin. Bueno Drops enables artists and creators of all levels to easily convert their artworks into digital collectibles (NFTs) without any coding skills. My work focused on simplifying complex NFT creation processes, ensuring that users could turn their Figma designs into digital assets in just a few clicks.

### Key Responsibilities:

* **User Experience (UX) Design**: Conducted user research to identify pain points in NFT creation. I focused on streamlining the process by developing intuitive user flows that guide creators from uploading their art to launching their drops with ease.

* **User Interface (UI) Design**: Designed a clean, modern interface in Figma that aligns with Bueno’s brand. The layout was tailored for creators at any skill level, incorporating minimal steps and clear, interactive elements. Key features such as drop type selection (Open or Limited Editions) were made visually accessible to provide clear distinctions between the available options.

* **Figma Integration**: Collaborated with the development team to ensure seamless integration with Figma. My design made it possible for users to export their Figma artboards directly into NFTs, providing real-time previews and a simple, no-code solution for NFT minting.

* **Drop Type Design**: Created unique visual cues and user interactions for Open and Limited Editions. For Open Editions, the design emphasized flexibility with unlimited supply, while Limited Editions showcased exclusivity by highlighting scarcity and collectible limits.

### Impact:

* Enabled creators to launch NFTs in under a minute, significantly reducing the complexity and time needed to create digital collectibles.

* Improved accessibility, making NFT creation viable for both new and seasoned artists through a no-code, highly visual platform.

* Boosted user engagement and satisfaction by focusing on a seamless, frictionless experience from design to drop.

Through careful attention to user needs and iterative design improvements, I helped make Bueno Drops a simple, effective tool for artists to bring their creations into the digital space.
